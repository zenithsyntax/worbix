Worbix Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Data Collection

Worbix collects minimal data necessary for app functionality:

  • Game progress and level completion data
  • Coins and achievements
  • Settings preferences

All data is stored locally on your device.

Data Storage

All your game data, including progress, coins, and unlocked levels, is stored locally on your device using secure local storage. We do not transmit your data to external servers.

Clear Data

You can clear all your data at any time through the Settings menu. This will permanently delete:

  • All game progress
  • All coins and achievements
  • All unlocked levels

This action cannot be undone.

Third-Party Services

Worbix uses third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) that may collect anonymous usage data for ad personalization. You can manage ad preferences through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Worbix is designed to be safe for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. All data collection is limited to what is necessary for game functionality.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
